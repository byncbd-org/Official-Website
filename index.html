About Bangladesh Youth Nuclear Congress

Founded June 2025 — Bangladesh's first and only youth-led nuclear organisation, and the official national partner of IYNC.

🎯 Our Mission

To unite, empower and internationally represent the young generation of nuclear science professionals and students in Bangladesh — working at the intersection of nuclear innovation, youth empowerment and the UN Sustainable Development Goals.

🔭 Our Vision

A Bangladesh where a skilled, globally-connected youth nuclear community drives the country's clean-energy transition and leads the post-Rooppur nuclear future on the world stage.

💡 Core Values
Knowledge TransferBridging senior scientists and the next generation through workshops and IYNC programming.
Peaceful Nuclear UseAdvocating strictly for civilian, clean-energy applications of nuclear science.
Global RepresentationGiving Bangladesh a clear, professional voice in international youth nuclear networks.
Innovation & SDGsRunning I4N to push student-led nuclear problem-solving onto the global stage.

❓ FAQ
Who can join BYNC? ▾

Any student or young professional (under 35) in nuclear science, nuclear engineering or a related discipline in Bangladesh. Students from MIST, Dhaka University and BUET are especially encouraged.

How do I get selected for the IYNC delegation? ▾

Delegation selection is competitive — based on I4N performance, academic background and overall contribution to BYNC. Top performers at I4N Bangladesh 2026 are strongly prioritised.

Is BYNC formally registered? ▾

BYNC operates as a youth nuclear network under academic supervision, and is the official national partner of IYNC. Formal registration is in progress.

Can companies partner with BYNC? ▾

Yes. We offer MOU-based partnerships and sponsorship roles for organisations in energy, technology and beyond. See the Partners page or contact us.
